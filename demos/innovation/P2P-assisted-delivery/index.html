Streamroot offers a state-of-the-art peer-accelerated streaming solution for online broadcasters. Combining traditional unicast delivery and P2P protocols, Streamroot offloads demand from servers by safely and securely connecting viewers watching streams simultaneously. It helps VOD and live streaming platforms scale naturally to growing audiences, better handle traffic spikes and offer better quality streams by delivering video from the source that can provide it mostly quickly.


Using JW Player’s modular framework to create our own custom provider, Streamroot offers a plug-and-play integration with JW Player. Our technology is plugin-free for viewers and integrates seamlessly into video platforms’ existing workflows.
